Why Math Facts?

Practice and repetition can help children learn basic arthimetic facts. These facts serve as the building blocks for future learning.

How to use these tests

With practice, children as young as seven years old can complete fifty math facts in about three minutes. Start with the simplest tests, and move to more difficult facts when easier ones are mastered.

The Math Facts story

When my son was in first grade, he had to answer fifty math facts in two and a half minutes. Once we ran out of the practice tests provided his school, we needed a way to create new tests.
